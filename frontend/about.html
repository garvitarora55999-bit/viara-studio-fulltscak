Our Maison

The story behind every facet, every curve, every gleam of gold.

2012 Our Origin

Born of Passion,
Built on Precision

Viara Studios was established with a singular conviction: that jewellery should be more than adornment — it should be a vessel for emotion, memory, and identity. Our founders, trained under Europe's most respected maîtres joailliers, set out to create a maison that honored tradition while embracing the future.

From a small atelier in the heart of Europe, we have grown into an internationally recognized house whose pieces grace the collections of discerning individuals across the globe, each creation carrying the unmistakable signature of Viara craftsmanship.

⚒️

Masterful Craft

Every piece is shaped by hands that have devoted decades to the art of goldsmithing. We employ techniques passed down through generations — hand-engraving, lost-wax casting, and micro-pavé setting — ensuring that each creation is a singular work of art.

🔬

Uncompromising Quality

We source only 18-karat gold and beyond, pair our pieces exclusively with VVS-clarity diamonds, and subject every finished creation to a rigorous multi-stage quality review before it earns the Viara hallmark.

🤝

Personal Connection

Jewellery marks life's most significant moments. Our concierge team provides a deeply personal experience — from initial consultation through delivery and beyond — ensuring every client feels the care behind the craft.

Responsibility

Jewellery with
a Conscience

Every gemstone in a Viara creation is ethically sourced through our network of certified mines and suppliers. We maintain full traceability from origin to atelier, ensuring that our beauty never comes at the cost of people or planet.

Our commitment extends to our operations: carbon-neutral shipping, recycled precious metals, and zero-waste packaging are just some of the principles that guide our daily practice. We believe luxury and responsibility are not opposing forces — they are inseparable.
