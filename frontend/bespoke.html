The Bespoke Process

From dream to reality — a guided experience of discovery, design, and devotion to craft.

01

Discovery

A private consultation — in person or virtual — to understand your vision, occasion, and aesthetic preferences. We explore inspirations, materials, and possibilities together.

02

Design & Refine

Our designers create hand-drawn sketches and 3D renderings for your review. We refine every detail — from gemstone selection to clasp engineering — until every element is perfect.

Our master goldsmiths bring the approved design to life over 6–10 weeks. Upon completion, your piece is delivered in signature Viara packaging with full documentation.

Behind This Project

Crafted by Garvit Arora

Designer & Developer

Viara Studios is a passion project that I, Garvit Arora, conceived, designed, and developed from the ground up. What began as a creative challenge — to build a luxury brand experience that felt genuinely premium, immersive, and emotionally resonant — evolved into one of the most rewarding projects I've ever worked on.

Every animation timing, every colour value, every typographic decision was deliberate. I wanted visitors to feel the weight of gold, the shimmer of a gemstone, the quiet confidence of true luxury — all through pixels and code. The process pushed me to think like a jeweller: obsessing over details invisible to most, but felt by everyone.

"Building Viara taught me that great design isn't about decoration — it's about intention. Every pixel should earn its place, just like every facet on a diamond."

From the scroll-triggered reveal animations to the responsive arch-shaped card layouts, from the particle system in the hero to the functional filter and sort on the boutique page — this project represents a complete front-end journey. It was an incredible experience to bring a fictional brand to life with the same care a real maison would demand.
