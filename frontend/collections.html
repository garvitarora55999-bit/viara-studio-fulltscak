Our Collections

Three distinct expressions of artistry — each inspired by light, form, and the poetry of precious materials.

I
Everyday Elegance

Aura Minimal

Refined simplicity for the modern connoisseur. The Aura Minimal collection distills luxury to its purest essence — clean lines, luminous metals, and singular gemstone accents that speak with quiet confidence.

Designed for everyday wear, each piece balances delicacy with durability, crafted from 18k gold with ethically sourced stones that catch the light at every turn.

High Jewellery

Royal Pavilion

Architectural grandeur rendered in precious metal. The Royal Pavilion collection draws from Gothic arches, celestial geometry, and the bold silhouettes of medieval craftsmanship — reimagined for the contemporary stage.

Statement pieces forged with exacting precision, featuring intricate lattice work, sculptural forms, and a commanding presence that demands attention.

Evening Statements

Nocturne

Born from shadow and fire. The Nocturne collection explores the drama of contrast — dark oxidized metals embrace vivid, heart-shaped resin stones in a dance between the visceral and the refined.

Designed for evenings that demand presence, these matching pairs and singular pendants carry an emotional weight that transcends conventional adornment.
