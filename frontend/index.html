Savoir-Faire

The Art of Creation

From initial sketch to final polish, every piece traverses a journey of uncompromising precision.

01

Conception

Hand-drawn sketches capture the vision. Our designers study proportion, light, and movement to perfect every angle.

02

Stone Selection

Each gemstone is individually chosen. We evaluate cut, clarity, colour, and character from ethically certified sources.

03

Master Setting

Our artisans employ centuries-old techniques — pavé, bezel, and prong — to secure each stone with flawless precision.

04

Final Polish

Every surface is hand-finished to achieve a mirror-like brilliance before receiving the Viara hallmark of quality.
